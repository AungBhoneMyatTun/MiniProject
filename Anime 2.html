  • Rating: 8.8/10
  • Story by: Junki Takegami,Hirohiko Kamisaka,Shōji Yonemura
  • Distributed by Toei Animation
  • Season: 20
  • Episodes: 1023
  • Brief Series Review: Monkey D. Luffy wants to become the King of all pirates. Along his quest he meets: a skilled swordsman named Roronoa Zolo; Nami, a greedy thief who has a knack for navigation; Usopp, a great liar who has an affinity for inventing; Sanji, a warrior cook; Chopper, a sentient deer who is also a skilled physician; and Robin, former member of Baroque Works. The gang sets sail to unknown seas in Grand Line to find the treasure of One Piece.

ATTACK ON TITAN 進撃の巨人

  • Title: Shingeki no Kyojin
  • Movie Type: Action,Fantasy,Romance,Adventure,Crime
  • Release Date: April 7, 2013(Japan)
  • Director: Tetsurō Araki,Masashi Koizuka,Yuichiro Hayashi,Jun Shishido
  • Rating: 9/10
  • Story by: Yasuko Kobayashi,Hiroshi Seko,Shintarō Kawakubo
  • Distributed by: Wit Studio,MAPPA
  • Season: 4
  • Episodes: 87
  • Brief Movie Review: When man-eating Titans first appeared 100 years ago, humans found safety behind massive walls that stopped the giants in their tracks. During the carnage that follows, soldier Eren Jaeger sees one of the creatures devour his mother, which leads him to vow that he will kill every Titan. He enlists some friends who survived to help him, and that group is humanity's last hope for avoiding extinction at the hands of the monsters.
